Mess Management System UP POLICE

UP Police Mess Management Project   A secure, efficient, and digitalized platform designed to streamline daily mess registrations, menu schedules, attendance logs, and monthly bills for police personnel. The purpose of this project is to ensure that all police personnel serving in police lines and police stations across Uttar Pradesh receive high‑quality meals, with regular monitoring and supervision of food standards. Guided by the principle that a healthy body contributes to building a healthy society, the system emphasizes transparency, accountability, and improved well‑being for those who serve.
